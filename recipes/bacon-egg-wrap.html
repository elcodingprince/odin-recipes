Bacon Egg Wrap

Description

The Bacon Egg Wrap I put together is perfect for maintaining the muscle you worked hard to gain and lose fat so that you could reach that lean physique you bean dreaming of

This amazing wrap comes in at only 310 calories with 30 gram of protein!

Ingredients

  • Carb Balance Flour Soft Taco Tortilla by Mission
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 slices of bacon
  • 1 egg white from large egg

Steps

  1. Cook 2 strips of bacon then put to side on napkin to drain oils off
  2. Scramble your 2 whole eggs and 1 egg white
  3. Heat up tortilla until satisfied
  4. Finally lay eggs and bacon on top and wrap it up!
  5. Optional: add low/zero calorie sauce.
