Chicken Wrap

Description

When trying to diet down to a lower body fat percentages its important to keep your calories for each meal low reletive to your total caloric intake for the day

For example my current caloric intake for the day is around 1700 so if im only eating 4 meals a day, I dont want any of those meals going over 425 calories

This chicken wrap only comes in at 328 calories which is perfect for me. This allows me to a bigger meal later for dinner.

Ingredients

  • Carb Balance Flour Soft Taco Tortilla by Mission
  • 102 grams of salted and fried chicken breast
  • 57 gram of avocado

Steps

  1. Heat up tortilla until satisfied
  2. Spread sliced up chicken evenly on top
  3. Spread the avocado evenly accross on top of your chicken
  4. Optional: add low/zero calorie sauce. I like to add mustard and siracha to mine
